<h1 align="center">📦 Samsung Supply Chain Analysis using Power BI</h1>

<p align="center">
An end-to-end supply chain analytics project focused on transforming Samsung supply chain data into actionable business insights using Power BI.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
This project analyzes Samsung supply chain operations to uncover insights related to supplier performance, inventory management, shipment efficiency, and customer sales trends.
The analysis is performed using <b>Power BI</b> through interactive dashboards, KPI tracking, and business intelligence reporting.
</p>

<h2>🏗️ Dashboard Structure</h2>
<ul>
<li><b>Main Dashboard:</b> Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard</li>
<li><b>Modules:</b></li>
<ul>
  <li><b>Overview Dashboard</b> – Overall supply chain KPIs and performance summary</li>
  <li><b>Supplier Analysis</b> – Supplier quality score, lead time, order quantity, and cost analysis</li>
  <li><b>Inventory Analysis</b> – Inventory value, stock levels, safety stock, and turnover analysis</li>
  <li><b>Shipment Analysis</b> – Shipment cost, delay tracking, carrier performance, and delivery analysis</li>
  <li><b>Customer Analysis</b> – Revenue, profit, discounts, and sales performance insights</li>
</ul>


<p>
The dashboard structure helps in efficient KPI monitoring and performance analysis.
</p>

<h2>🧹 Data Cleaning & Transformation</h2>
<ul>
<li>Handled missing and null values</li>
<li>Validated business data consistency</li>
<li>Created relationships between tables</li>
<li>Transformed raw data using Power Query</li>
<li>Built DAX measures for KPI calculations</li>
</ul>

<h2>📈 Key Analysis Performed</h2>

<li>Shipment Delay & Delivery Performance Analysis</li>
